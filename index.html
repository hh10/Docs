Biography

I am a PhD candidate in the Verification of Autonomous Systems group led by Prof. Alessio Lomuscio in the Department of Computing, Imperial College London. I am working on verifying neural networks against semantic changes in image domain using generative modelling. I am also interested in defining and verifying semantic properties in language domain, multiagent social settings, etc.

Before joining Imperial, I was working at Daedalean AG, the coolest place to be at for working towards developing certifiable autonomous autopilot for manned aerial vehicles.
